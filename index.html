Hello! My name is Alfena, but I go by Alfy for short.
I am currently a second degree bachelor's student at UMass Amherst in the Veterinary Technology program with a Natural Resource Conservation minor, and my first bachelors is in Scientific Illustration.

I have a passion for wildlife, and I want to direct it towards conservation efforts. As humans, we are the stewards of this planet. I believe that we have a duty to not just protect the planet, but live in harmony with the resources it provides us. As stewards, every human should have access to the wonders that are living in their own communities. No matter where you live, whether its the city, the country or the sea, you can make a difference.

The easiest way to start is to just learn more about the environment, but a lot of people aren't able to have a relationship with the earth due to language, learning, socioeconomic, and more intersectional barriers that prevent them from engaging with science. My goal through my art and my career is to make science and learning about the environment more approachable and easy to learn for people from varied backgrounds. Conservation is nothing without a community that is willing to enact change, and it is integral to conservation efforts to bring scientific communication to a diversity of communities.
